Privacy Policy - RoomRadar

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Last Updated: August 31, 2025

1. Introduction

At RoomRadar, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

RoomRadar operates as an intelligent real estate listing aggregation system across multiple cities, helping users find rental properties by collecting and organizing publicly available rental listings.

2. Who We Are

Service Information

Service Name: RoomRadar

Purpose: Intelligent real estate listing aggregation system operating across multiple cities

Data Controller: RoomRadar

Privacy Contact Email: hello@roomradar.app

Website: https://roomradar.app

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Automated Collection from Public Sources

  • Public Facebook posts from rental groups via RSS feeds
  • Images from public rental posts
  • Publicly available contact information (phone numbers, emails)
  • Rental information (prices, locations, descriptions, property details)
  • Location data related to rental properties across our operating cities

3.2 User Account Data (When You Register)

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • User preferences and search criteria
  • Account creation and login timestamps
  • Password (stored securely and encrypted)

3.3 Analytics and Usage Data

  • Website usage statistics and patterns
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP addresses and general location data
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platform
  • Search queries and preferences

3.4 Important Note About Public Data

We only collect data that is already publicly available on Facebook groups and other public platforms. We do not collect private or personal data beyond what is already accessible to the public. We do not access private messages, personal profiles, or non-public information.

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Primary Services

  • Aggregate and organize rental listings from multiple cities
  • Analyze and filter relevant housing posts
  • Provide reliability ratings and quality assessments for listings
  • Display rental information in an organized, searchable format
  • Customize search results based on user preferences

4.2 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
  • Monitor platform performance and reliability
  • Develop new features and expand to additional cities
  • Ensure data quality and remove outdated listings

4.3 Communication

  • Send service-related notifications to registered users
  • Respond to support inquiries and feedback
  • Notify users of significant changes to our service

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Under GDPR, we process data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing housing search services and processing publicly available information
  • Consent: When you create an account or subscribe to notifications
  • Contract Performance: When providing services to registered users

6. Data Protection and Privacy Measures

6.1 Automatic Privacy Protection

  • Phone numbers are automatically censored in public displays
  • Email addresses are hidden from public view
  • Personal identifiers are masked or anonymized
  • WhatsApp/Telegram contacts are anonymized
  • Contact information is only revealed to verified users when appropriate

6.2 Technical Security Measures

  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Automated data cleanup processes

7. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We work with the following trusted third-party services:

7.1 Essential Services

  • Firebase (Google): User authentication and database storage
  • RSS.app: RSS feed processing for Facebook group data
  • Notion: Internal data organization and management
  • OpenRouter/AI Services: Content analysis and listing quality assessment

7.2 Infrastructure and Development

  • Cloud Hosting Services: Secure cloud infrastructure for platform operations
  • Code Repository Services: Version control and development workflow management

7.3 Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Vercel Analytics: Platform performance and user experience metrics

7.4 Data Sharing Policy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared with the services listed above as necessary to operate our platform, and all third-party services are required to maintain appropriate data protection standards.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your data
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision Making: Request human review of automated decisions

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@roomradar.app.

9. Data Retention

9.1 Public Listing Data

  • Rental listings are automatically removed after 30 days
  • Cached listing data is regularly cleaned and updated
  • Historical data may be retained in anonymized form for service improvement

9.2 User Account Data

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • You may request account deletion at any time
  • Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law

9.3 Analytics Data

  • Analytics data is typically retained for 24-38 months
  • Usage statistics may be retained in anonymized form indefinitely

10. International Data Transfers

As we operate across multiple cities and use international service providers (Google, Vercel, etc.), your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for all international transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Privacy certification programs
