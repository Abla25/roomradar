1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how RoomRadar uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

RoomRadar is an AI-powered housing search platform that helps users find rental properties in Barcelona, London, and Rome. We use cookies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

3. How We Use Cookies

RoomRadar uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Help us provide relevant housing-related content

4. Types of Cookies We Use

4.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include:

  • Authentication cookies for user login
  • Security cookies to protect against fraud
  • Session management cookies
  • Cookie consent preferences

4.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users search for housing and interact with our AI-powered recommendations:

  • Google Analytics cookies to track site usage
  • Vercel Analytics for performance monitoring
  • User behavior analysis for platform improvements

4.3 Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us provide better housing search experiences:

  • Content personalization based on search preferences
  • Platform improvement effectiveness measurement
  • User experience optimization
