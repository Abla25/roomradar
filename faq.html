Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RoomRadar and how we help you find the perfect accommodation in Barcelona and Rome.

How does RoomRadar work?
RoomRadar uses AI to scan Facebook groups and housing portals in Barcelona and Rome, filters out scams, and rates listings for reliability. You choose your city, set your preferences, and we show you the best options with trust ratings.
Is RoomRadar free to use?
Yes! During our beta phase, RoomRadar is completely free for all users. We're focused on building the best possible experience and gathering feedback from students in Barcelona and Rome. Once we launch publicly, we'll maintain a free tier with premium features available.
What cities does RoomRadar cover?
RoomRadar currently covers Barcelona and Rome. You can choose your preferred city when setting up your search preferences. We plan to expand to other European university cities soon.
How accurate are the AI trust ratings?
Our AI analyzes multiple factors including post completeness, photo quality, landlord communication patterns, and price consistency. While no system is perfect, our trust ratings have shown 85% accuracy in identifying legitimate listings in both cities. We continuously improve our algorithms based on user feedback and real-world results.
How do I get started with RoomRadar?
Simply join our beta by signing up on our homepage. You'll receive an email with your access credentials within 24 hours. Once logged in, you can choose your city (Barcelona or Rome), set your preferences, budget, and preferred areas. Our AI will then start finding and rating listings that match your criteria.
How often are new listings added?
Our AI scans for new listings every few hours, so you'll see fresh options throughout the day. You can also set up notifications to get instant alerts when new listings match your criteria in your chosen city, ensuring you're among the first to know about new opportunities.
What if I find a scam or fake listing?
We have a "Report Listing" feature on every listing page. When you report a suspicious listing, our team reviews it immediately and updates our AI algorithms to better detect similar scams in the future. Your feedback helps make RoomRadar safer for everyone.
Can I use RoomRadar if I'm not a student?
Currently, RoomRadar is designed specifically for students and young professionals looking for shared housing or student-friendly accommodations in Barcelona and Rome. However, many of the listings we find are suitable for anyone looking for affordable housing.
How can I provide feedback or suggest features?
We love feedback! You can join our Telegram community to share ideas, report bugs, or suggest new features. We also send regular surveys to beta users to understand what's working and what needs improvement. Your input directly shapes the future of RoomRadar.
